How ambient factors affect student's performance


Introduce
The grading system was always the key component utilized to measure the pupils' level, competence, or even IQ throughout our schooling process. However, does a student's grade accurately reflect their ability or intelligence? We'd say it does occasionally, but not most of the time. This project was developed to aid in the visualization and discovery of trends and links between a student's performance and background.
